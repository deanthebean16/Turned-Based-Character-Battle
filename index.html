Maphean


-OVERVIEW-

Welcome to Maphean - a game that integrates fighting and gambling. Once players have loaded into the game, they can get characters by using the gacha option. Once they have a character they are satisfied with, they can use them to enter the Colosseum! To win, you will have to beat all the opponents that stand before you in turn-based combat and become the champion! If you lose, do not fret; you can always try again!


-CHARACTER-

To choose a character, click the button in the top right corner. This will bring you to the Gacha page where you can roll for a specific character. Here are the rates:

S tier characters: 5% chance (Gojo, Mumen Rider)
A tier characters: 10% chance (Zoro, Saber, Ichigo)
B tier characters: 35% chance (Eren, Hisoka, Erza)
C tier characters: 50% chance (Roy Mustang, Sogeking, Megumin)


-BATTLE-

Once you have picked a character, you can now step into the arena! The fight is turn-based, and you start first. There are two options to pick during your turn: Attack and Parry.

Attack: Has a chance to deal damage equal to your ATK at a chance of your HIT%.
Parry: Has a chance to block your opponent's incoming damage for your own ATK and also sets it to your turn again. Note, though, that parry takes account on both your PARRY% and the opponent's HIT%, and the chance will be much lower. Once you have beaten all opponents, you will be able to see the ending...
